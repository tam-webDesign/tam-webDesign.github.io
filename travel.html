Venice, Italy

A striking view of a canal in Venice, Italy.

Description

Venice is arguably one of the most picturesque tourist destinations in the world. It is famous for its beautiful canals, stunning architecture, and rich history. Combining all of this with its friendly and energetic culure, this city makes itself a great place to explore and experience a unique and magical atmosphere.

Places you can visit in Venice:

  • St. Mark's Square
  • the Rialto Bridge
  • Grand Canal

Rio de Janerio is definitely a tourist destination with a lot to offer. The city is profoundly resonant in colors, sounds, and flavors.
